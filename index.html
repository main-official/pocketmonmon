How to Play

  • 🥚 Tap the egg to hatch it, then give your salmon a name.
  • 🖐️ Press and drag anywhere in the water — your salmon eases toward your finger or cursor.
  • ☝️ While dragging, swipe upward for your salmon to leap out with a splash!
  • 🌊 Let go and it'll cruise around on its own.
  • 📈 Every 14 real-world days your salmon grows into its next life stage: Alevin → Fry → Parr → Smolt → Adult Salmon.
  • ⚙️ Use the gear icon if you want to skip ahead in days or start over with a new egg.
