How to play

  • Hatch it: tap the egg to hatch your salmon on Day 0.
  • Name it: give your new fish a name.
  • Watch it swim: it cruises around the tank on its own.
  • Guide it: press and drag anywhere in the water and it'll follow your finger or cursor.
  • Make it jump: swipe up quickly to send it leaping out of the water with a splash.
  • Watch it grow: every 14 real-world days it advances a life stage — Alevin → Fry → Parr → Smolt → Adult Salmon.

Your progress saves automatically in this browser, so it'll be right where you left it next time you visit.
